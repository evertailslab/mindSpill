What WARDWISE Does

Everything nursing education needs — in one platform.

52 Clinical Scenarios
Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced cases across Cardiac, Respiratory, Neurological, Sepsis, Renal, OB/GYN, and 4 more specialty domains — all evidence-based.
Multi-Shift Progression
Scenarios unfold across Day 1, Day 2, and Day 3. Patient vitals and condition change in real time based on what each student actually decided.
Intelligent Scoring
Every clinical decision is scored: +4 excellent, +3 good, +1 acceptable, −2 harmful. Immediate feedback explains the clinical reasoning behind every score.
Analytics Dashboard
Individual and cohort-level performance tracking, common error analysis, trend charts, and Excel export — HRIS-formatted and ready for Oracle Fusion and Workday.
Cohort Management
Create cohorts, bulk-assign scenarios, and manage hundreds of students. Role-based access for instructors, org admins, and institution-level administrators.
Standards-Aligned
Scenarios built on 2026 AHA, ANA, ACOG, KDIGO, and SSC guidelines. NMBA-aligned assessment scoring. DOH-compatible for UAE institutional competency reporting.
How It Works

Three steps. Real outcomes.

1
Set up your cohort
Create your institution, add students, and assign clinical scenarios matched to your curriculum level and specialty focus.
2
Students work through cases
Each student makes clinical decisions across 3-day multi-shift scenarios. The patient responds to every choice they make — good or bad.
3
Track, report, improve
Review analytics, identify learning gaps across your cohort, and export competency data for accreditation and HR systems.
